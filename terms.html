2. Description of Service

MediCabinet is a personal medicine cabinet tracker application for iOS that allows you to:

  • Track and organize medications, vitamins, supplements, and natural remedies
  • Set expiration date reminders and receive alerts
  • Search drug information from public government health databases
  • Sync your medication data across Apple devices via iCloud
  • Access a built-in drug encyclopedia with 70+ medications
