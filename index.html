Features

Everything You Need to Manage Your Medications

A powerful, privacy-first toolkit designed to help you organize your entire medicine cabinet, stay informed with trusted health data, and never miss an expiration date.

📦

Complete Medicine Cabinet

Add and organize all your medications, vitamins, supplements, herbal remedies, and homeopathic products in one centralized place. Track name, quantity, expiration date, storage location, category, type, and personal notes for every item in your cabinet.

⏰

Smart Expiration Alerts

Never use expired medicine again. MediCabinet sends you 4 automatic reminders: 30 days before, 7 days before, 1 day before, and on the expiration day at 9:00 AM. All notifications are generated locally on your device — no external servers involved.

🔍

Drug Encyclopedia

Browse a built-in database of 70+ common medications with detailed information. Search real-time data from three U.S. government health databases for drug labels, warnings, dosage info, side effects, interactions, and official package inserts.

☁️

Seamless iCloud Sync

Your medication data automatically syncs across all your Apple devices — iPhone and iPad — via your private iCloud database. Add a medication on your iPhone and instantly see it on your iPad. Your data is encrypted and accessible only to your Apple ID.

🏷️

14 Smart Categories

Organize medications across 14 categories: Pain Relief, Allergy, Digestive, Cold & Flu, Sleep, Vitamins, Supplements, First Aid, Skin Care, Herbal Tea, Herbal Supplement, Homeopathic, Aromatherapy, and Other. Filter and sort to find what you need instantly.

🔒

Private & Secure by Design

Your health data stays on your device and your private iCloud — period. No user accounts, no registration, no analytics, no ads, and no third-party tracking. We have no servers and cannot access your data. Built with Apple's industry-leading security and encryption.

📱

Works Offline

Your entire medicine cabinet works without an internet connection. Add, edit, view, and manage medications offline. The built-in encyclopedia with 70+ medications is always available. Internet is only needed for searching the government drug databases, and results are cached locally for future offline access.

⚡

Smart Caching

Drug information you search is automatically cached on your device for faster access next time. Configure refresh intervals — daily, weekly, biweekly, or monthly — or manage your cache manually. Search results are deduplicated to minimize network usage.

Trusted Data Sources

Powered by U.S. Government Health Databases

MediCabinet connects you directly to three authoritative, publicly available U.S. government health databases. All data is provided free of charge by federal agencies dedicated to public health and safety. No personal information is ever sent — only the drug name you search for.

🏛️

openFDA

U.S. Food & Drug Administration

Access official FDA drug labels, safety warnings, reported adverse events, and National Drug Code (NDC) directory information. openFDA provides public access to high-value FDA datasets, including detailed labeling data for thousands of medications approved in the United States.

Source: api.fda.gov — Public domain, free access

💊

RxNorm

National Library of Medicine (NLM)

Search standardized drug names, clinical identifiers (RxCUI), drug-to-drug interactions, and spelling suggestions. RxNorm is the standard nomenclature for clinical drugs in the United States, providing normalized names and linking between different drug vocabularies used in pharmacy, medicine, and research.

Source: rxnav.nlm.nih.gov — National Institutes of Health

📋

DailyMed

National Library of Medicine (NLM)

Access complete, official package inserts and Structured Product Labeling (SPL) documents submitted to the FDA by pharmaceutical companies. DailyMed provides the most up-to-date labeling information, including indications, dosage, contraindications, and detailed prescribing information.

Source: dailymed.nlm.nih.gov — National Institutes of Health
